Write a program that takes two numbers as input. One is the amount (money) given and the other is the charged amount (money). Your main task is to give back the change (money)  to the user which would be the difference between the given amount and the charged amount.  Your program should return each type of bill (currency notes) and coins (currency coins) to the user according to your country. Try to give as few bills and coins as possible to the user.
