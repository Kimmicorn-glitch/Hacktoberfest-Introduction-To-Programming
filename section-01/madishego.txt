writing a program that checks for job clashes and priorities jobs with higher weight and once the 
prior job is completed the next one will be open.
input from user:
-job number
-job weight
-start time 
-end time

solving:
compare job numbers if the number is the first job
check for the start time and end times
compare end time of first job to the start time of the next job
allocate job to the user if there are no time clashes and the first job is completed.

output:
-if clashes-"Finish the first job"
-if no clashes-"Next job allocated and start time is {start time}
